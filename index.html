Delicious Soup Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup diced potatoes
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, saut√© onions and garlic until golden brown.
  2. Add carrots, celery, and potatoes. Cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Pour in the broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious soup!
